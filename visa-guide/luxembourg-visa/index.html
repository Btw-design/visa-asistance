What is a Luxembourg Visa?

Luxembourg is a Schengen Area member, allowing Indian travellers with a Luxembourg-issued Schengen visa to visit all 27 Schengen countries. Indian citizens need to apply at the Luxembourg Embassy through VFS Global if Luxembourg is the primary destination or the first point of entry.

Luxembourg is one of the smaller Schengen countries but an important financial and diplomatic hub. It hosts numerous EU institutions and is a popular destination for business travellers, conferences, and tourists exploring the Benelux region. Over 10,000 Indians visit Luxembourg annually for business and tourism purposes.

Do Indians Need a Visa for Luxembourg?

Yes, Indian passport holders need a Schengen visa to enter Luxembourg for any purpose including tourism, business, and transit.

Documents Required for Luxembourg Visa

Every Luxembourg visa application requires proof of identity, purpose of visit, and financial capacity.

Core Documents (All Visa Types)

  • Valid Indian passport — Must have at least 6 months validity beyond intended stay. At least 2 blank pages.
  • Digital photograph — 35x45mm, white background, taken within 6 months.
  • Completed online application — Via the official portal. All details must match your passport exactly.
  • Previous visas and travel history — Include copies of previous visas and travel stamps.
  • Cover letter — Explaining purpose of visit, duration, and itinerary.

Short Stay Schengen Visa — Schengen Type C

Best for: Tourism, business, transit, family visit.

Additional documents: Flight itinerary, accommodation bookings, travel insurance with €30,000 minimum coverage, bank statements for last 3 months, employment letter with salary details, leave approval from employer.

Long Stay Visa — Type D (Visa de Long Sejour)

Best for: Study, work, family reunion, long stay.

Additional documents: Admission letter (for students) or work contract (for employees), proof of accommodation in Luxembourg, police clearance certificate, medical certificate, proof of sufficient funds for entire stay duration.

Student Visa — VLS-TS Etudiant

Best for: Pursuing higher education in Luxembourg.

Additional documents: Admission letter from a recognised Luxembourg institution, proof of language proficiency (English/French/German), education loan sanction letter or sponsor documents, hostel or accommodation proof, no objection certificate from previous institution if applicable.

Work Visa — VLS-TS Salarie

Best for: Employment with a Luxembourg company.

Additional documents: Valid employment contract authorised by Luxembourg's Ministry of Labour, employer sponsorship letter, qualification certificates, professional experience letters, proof of accommodation.

Business Schengen Visa — Schengen Type C

Best for: Business meetings, EU conferences, trade fairs.

Additional documents: Invitation letter from Luxembourg company or organisation, company registration proof, ITR for last 2 years, business bank statements, previous business travel history, purpose of visit explanation.

Why Financial Evidence Matters

Immigration authorities need assurance that you can support yourself without working illegally or relying on public funds. Incomplete or inconsistent financial documentation is a leading cause of refusal for Indian applicants.

Luxembourg Visa Application Process for Indians

The application process typically involves the following steps:

1
Choose Your Visa Type and Check Eligibility

Use the official visa finder or this guide to select the correct visa category. Check eligibility requirements including financial capacity and supporting documents.

2
Complete the Online Application Form

Fill out the online visa application form on the Luxembourg immigration portal with accurate personal details, passport information, and travel history. Any discrepancy can lead to refusal.

3
Pay the Visa Fee

Pay the non-refundable visa fee online via credit/debit card. Your bank will apply the current exchange rate plus forex markup.

4
Schedule Appointment at VFS Global

Book an appointment at your nearest VFS Global visa application centre. Luxembourg has no consulates in India — all applications are processed through VFS Global and sent to the Embassy in New Delhi. Centres available in Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Cochin, and Chandigarh.

5
Submit Documents and Biometrics

Visit the VFS centre on your appointment date with all required documents. Provide fingerprints and photograph at the centre. Walk-ins are not accepted.

6
Wait for Processing and Track Online

Processing times vary by visa category. Track your application status online using the reference number provided by VFS Global.

7
Receive Decision and Collect Passport

If approved, your passport will be stamped with the Schengen visa. Collect your passport from the VFS centre or have it couriered to your address.

Travel Insurance Requirements

Travel insurance is mandatory for all Schengen visa applications to Luxembourg:

  • Tourist/Business Visitors: Comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies up to €30,000, trip cancellation, and baggage loss is mandatory for Schengen visa approval.
  • Students: Health insurance is mandatory for student visa holders. Coverage must meet the minimum requirements set by the university or immigration authorities.
  • Work Visa Holders: Employer-provided health coverage is typically required and must be registered with Luxembourg's social security system.
  • Coverage Area: Insurance must be valid across the entire Schengen Area, not just Luxembourg.

Common reasons for Luxembourg visa rejection for Indian applicants:

  • Insufficient funds — bank statements must show adequate balance (€50/day minimum).
  • Incomplete documentation — missing or incorrect documents.
  • Weak travel history — limited international travel history may lead to stricter scrutiny.
  • Discrepancies between application form and supporting documents.
  • Unclear travel purpose — vague or unconvincing cover letter explaining the visit.
  • Previous visa violations — overstays or immigration violations in Schengen or other countries.

Visa extension information for Luxembourg:

  • Schengen short-stay visas can only be extended in exceptional circumstances (force majeure, humanitarian reasons, or serious personal reasons).
  • Apply before your current visa expires to avoid overstaying.
  • Applications must be made to the Luxembourg immigration authorities.
  • Extension fees and processes vary; supporting documents justifying the extension must be provided.
  • Overstaying can result in fines and future visa complications.

Language information for Luxembourg:

  • English is widely spoken in business settings and tourist areas, especially in Luxembourg City.

Cost considerations for Indian tourists visiting Luxembourg:
