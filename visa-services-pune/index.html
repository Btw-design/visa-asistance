FRRO Services for Foreign Nationals in Pune

  • Foreigner Registration (FR)

  • Exit Permit
  • Residential Permit

Who Needs FRRO Registration?

Foreign nationals entering India on a long-term visa (valid for more than 180 days) must register with the FRRO within 14 days of arrival. Pakistani nationals must register within 24 hours of arrival.

Reference: Bureau of Immigration (boi.gov.in), Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visa services in Pune.

The visa application process from Pune typically involves: determining the correct visa type for your travel purpose, preparing the required documents, filling the online application form, scheduling an appointment at the respective visa application center (VFS Global or BLS International), submitting biometrics and documents, and awaiting processing. Our visa assistance team guides you through each step.

Pune has multiple authorized visa application centers. VFS Global operates centers in Wakad and Baner, handling visa applications for countries including the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and Schengen nations. BLS International has a center on JM Road, Shivajinagar, processing visa applications for Spain, Portugal, and other countries.

Common documents required for most visa applications include: a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, completed visa application form, recent passport-size photographs, travel itinerary, flight and hotel bookings, bank statements for the last 3-6 months, income tax returns, employment letter or business proof, and travel insurance. Specific requirements vary by country and visa type.

Visa processing times vary by country. US visitor visas typically take 2-4 weeks, UK visitor visas 3-6 weeks, Schengen visas 2-4 weeks, Canada visitor visas 4-8 weeks, and Australia visitor visas 3-6 weeks. Processing times depend on the embassy workload, completeness of documents, and whether an interview is required.

For most visa categories, the initial application and biometric submission can be done at the visa application centers in Pune. However, certain visa types may require an in-person interview at the respective embassy or consulate in Mumbai. This includes US visa interviews and some Schengen visa categories. Our team provides guidance on interview preparation and scheduling.

VFS Global and BLS International are authorized visa application centers that act as intermediaries between applicants and embassies. VFS Global handles applications for countries including the UK, US, Canada, Australia, and Schengen nations. BLS International primarily handles visa applications for Spain, Portugal, and select other countries. Both centers collect documents, capture biometrics, and forward applications to the respective embassies for processing.

The Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) in Pune handles registration and visa-related services for foreign nationals. Foreigners entering India on a long-term visa (valid for more than 180 days) must register with the FRRO within 14 days of arrival. The FRRO Pune office is located at Passport Bhawan, Baner-Pashan Link Road, Baner. Most services can also be accessed online through the e-FRRO portal at indianfrro.gov.in.
