CPHI Milan 2026: Your Complete Guide to the World's Largest Pharma Exhibition

Published: July 11, 2026 5 min read By Ajit Mane Updated 2026

If you work in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, or drug manufacturing, CPHI Milan 2026 is arguably the most important event on your calendar this year. Taking place from 6–8 October 2026 at Fiera Milano in Italy, this global exhibition brings together over 50,000 pharma professionals from more than 180 countries to network, discover innovations, and forge partnerships that shape the future of healthcare.

Whether you are a first-time attendee or a seasoned CPHI veteran, planning ahead is essential. From understanding the exhibition layout to securing your Italy business visa in time, this guide covers everything you need to make the most of your CPHI Milan experience.

Why CPHI Milan Matters

CPHI (Convention on Pharmaceutical Ingredients) has been the world's premier pharmaceutical exhibition for over three decades. Milan, as a global pharmaceutical hub, offers the perfect backdrop for this annual gathering. The event covers the entire pharma supply chain — from active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and excipients to finished dosage forms, packaging, and contract services.

For Indian pharma companies and professionals, CPHI Milan is especially significant. India is a leading supplier of APIs and generic drugs to the global market, and the exhibition provides a direct platform to connect with European buyers, explore regulatory updates from the European Medicines Agency (EMA), and stay competitive in an evolving industry.

Who Should Attend CPHI Milan 2026?

CPHI Milan is designed for professionals across the pharmaceutical ecosystem. You'll find value here if you are a:

  • Pharma manufacturer or supplier looking to showcase your products and find new buyers.
  • R&D professional seeking the latest innovations in drug development and delivery systems.
  • Regulatory affairs expert aiming to stay updated on global compliance standards.
  • Contract manufacturing or research organisation (CMO/CRO) networking for partnership opportunities.
  • Procurement or sourcing manager evaluating suppliers and negotiating contracts.
  • Business traveller exploring the European pharma market for expansion or collaboration.

Quick Stats

CPHI Milan 2026 expects 50,000+ attendees, 2,500+ exhibitors, and representation from 180+ countries across 10 exhibition halls at Fiera Milano.

Key Highlights of CPHI Milan 2026

1. Extensive Exhibition Floor

With over 2,500 exhibitors spanning across dedicated zones for APIs, excipients, finished dosage forms, packaging, biopharmaceuticals, and contract services, the exhibition floor is a treasure trove of opportunities. Each zone is colour-coded and clearly signposted, making navigation straightforward even with the sheer size of the event.

2. Conference & Seminar Programme

The CPHI conference track features keynote sessions from industry leaders, regulatory updates from agencies like EMA and FDA, and deep-dive seminars on topics including gene therapy manufacturing, sustainable pharma, digital supply chains, and AI in drug discovery. Pre-booking your sessions is recommended as seats fill quickly.

3. Networking Events

From the official CPHI networking reception to country pavilions and buyer meetings, the event is structured to facilitate meaningful business connections. The India Pavilion is always a major draw, showcasing the capabilities of Indian pharma companies on the global stage.

4. Innovation Awards

The CPHI Pharma Awards celebrate excellence in formulation, manufacturing, and sustainability. Attending the award ceremony is also a fantastic networking opportunity.

How to Prepare for CPHI Milan 2026

Preparation is the key to getting real value from a large exhibition. Here are practical steps to take:

1

Register Early

Early-bird registration opens months before the event. Registering early not only saves money but also gives you priority access to conference sessions and one-to-one buyer meetings.

2

Plan Your Schedule

Study the exhibitor list and conference programme in advance. Identify the booths you must visit, the sessions you want to attend, and the people you want to meet. Use the CPHI mobile app to build your personalised agenda.

3

Book Accommodation Near Fiera Milano

Hotels near the exhibition centre fill up quickly. Book your stay in the Rho-Fiera area or along the Milan metro line for convenient access. Consider sharing accommodation with colleagues to reduce costs.

4

Secure Your Schengen Business Visa Early

Indian nationals travelling to Italy for CPHI Milan will need a Schengen business visa. Visa appointment slots fill up fast during the exhibition season, so applying at least 6–8 weeks in advance is crucial. You will need a letter of invitation from the event organiser or an exhibiting company, confirmed travel insurance, hotel bookings, flight reservations, and proof of sufficient funds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When and where is CPHI Milan 2026?
A: CPHI Milan 2026 will be held from 6–8 October 2026 at Fiera Milano in Milan, Italy.

Q: Who organises CPHI Milan?
A: CPHI is organised by Informa Markets, a leading international events and publishing company.

Q: Can I get a visitor visa for CPHI Milan?
A: Yes. Indian nationals require a Schengen business visa (short-stay type C) to attend CPHI Milan. You will need an invitation letter, confirmed travel itinerary, hotel bookings, travel insurance, and financial documents.

Q: How much does it cost to attend CPHI Milan?
A: Registration fees vary depending on the pass type. An exhibition-only pass is typically less expensive than a full conference pass. Early-bird discounts are available.

Q: Is there an India Pavilion at CPHI Milan 2026?
A: Yes. The India Pavilion is a prominent feature, showcasing Indian pharmaceutical companies, API manufacturers, and contract service providers. It provides excellent networking opportunities for Indian attendees.

Q: What is the best time to apply for a Schengen business visa for Italy?
A: You can submit your application up to 6 months before your travel date. For CPHI Milan 2026 (October 6–8), apply by mid-August at the latest to secure an appointment slot before the peak season rush.
