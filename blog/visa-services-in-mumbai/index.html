Visa Application Centers in Mumbai

Mumbai hosts several visa application centres (VACs) and consulates that handle visa processing for different countries. Below is a complete list of visa application centres in Mumbai with their locations and the visa types they handle.

VFS Global Mumbai (BKC)

VFS Global is the official outsourcing partner for multiple governments and manages visa applications at its Mumbai centre. The centre accepts applications for the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and several European countries.

Address: Unit 101, 1st Floor, A Wing, TCG Financial Centre, Adjacent to Upscale Hotel, BKC, Bandra East, Mumbai — 400051

Visa Types Processed: UK Visitor Visa, Canada Visitor Visa, Australia Visitor Visa, New Zealand Visitor Visa, and several European national visas.

US Consulate General Mumbai & US Travel Docs

The US Consulate General in Mumbai processes nonimmigrant visa applications for residents of western and central India. All visa appointments are scheduled through the official US Travel Docs website.

Consulate Address: C-49, G-Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai — 400051

Visa Types: B-1/B-2 Tourist/Business, F-1 Student, H-1B Work, L-1 Intracompany Transfer, J-1 Exchange Visitor, and other nonimmigrant categories.

Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) Mumbai

While primarily for Indian passport services, the Passport Seva Kendra in Mumbai is essential for anyone needing a passport before applying for a visa. The Regional Passport Office in Mumbai operates several PSKs across the city.

Main RPO Address: Regional Passport Office, B-wing, 8th Floor, NSIL Shelton Cubix, Plot No. B-25 & B-26, Sector 15, CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai — 400614

Visit the official Passport Seva portal for appointments at PSK locations across Mumbai.

FRRO Mumbai (Foreigners Registration)

Foreign nationals arriving in India who need to stay beyond 180 days must register with the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) in Mumbai. The FRRO handles visa extensions, conversions, exit permits, and OCI/PIO services.

Address: Annexe-II, Commissariat of Police Building, Near Crawford Market, DN Road, Mumbai — 400001

All registrations and applications are processed through the official e-FRRO portal.

Step-by-Step Visa Application Process in Mumbai

The visa application process from Mumbai generally follows these steps regardless of your destination country. Specific requirements may vary by visa type.

Step 1: Determine Your Visa Type
Identify the correct visa category for your travel purpose. Use our Visa Guide or check the official embassy website to confirm eligibility requirements. Each visa type has specific documentation and financial requirements.

Step 2: Complete the Online Application Form
Most visa applications today begin online. Fill out the application form for your destination country on the official visa portal. Common portals include the DS-160 (USA), GOV.UK (UK), IRCC Portal (Canada), and ImmiAccount (Australia). Errors in the application form are a leading cause of delays, so double-check every entry against your passport.

Step 3: Pay the Visa Application Fee
Pay the non-refundable visa fee online using a credit or debit card. Some centres also accept demand drafts. Keep the payment receipt — you will need it for your appointment and later for tracking your application. See the fees section below for current visa costs.

Step 4: Schedule an Appointment
Book your appointment at the relevant visa application centre in Mumbai through the official scheduling portal (e.g., US Travel Docs for US visas, VFS Global for UK/Canada/Australia). Appointment availability varies by season — book well in advance during peak summer and December holiday periods.

Step 5: Attend the Appointment & Submit Biometrics
Visit the visa application centre on your scheduled date and time. Bring all required documents in original and photocopy. Most visa types require biometric data collection (fingerprints and photograph). The appointment typically takes 15–30 minutes.

Step 6: Track Your Application & Receive Decision
After submission, you can track your application status online using the reference number provided at the centre. Processing times vary by visa type and country (see table above). Once a decision is made, you will be notified to collect your passport or have it couriered to your address.

Documents Required for Visa Applications in Mumbai

While document requirements vary by visa type and destination country, the following are standard documents that most visa applications from Mumbai require:

  • Valid Passport — Must have at least 6 months validity beyond your intended stay, with at least 2 blank pages. If you need a new passport, apply at a Passport Seva Kendra through the official Passport Seva portal.
  • Completed Visa Application Form — Printed confirmation page with barcode from the respective country's online portal.
  • Passport-Sized Photographs — As per country-specific specifications (size, background colour, and recency). Most centres have photo booths on-site.
  • Proof of Financial Means — Bank statements for the last 3–6 months, salary slips, IT returns (typically 2–3 years), and fixed deposit certificates showing sufficient funds to cover your travel and stay.
  • Travel Itinerary — Round-trip flight bookings and hotel reservations for your entire stay. Do not purchase non-refundable tickets until the visa is approved.
  • Employment Documents — Employment verification letter, leave approval, pay slips (last 3–6 months). Self-employed applicants should provide business registration, GST returns, and CA-certified financial statements.
  • Cover Letter — A signed letter explaining the purpose of your visit, duration, itinerary, and how you will fund the trip.
  • Visa-Specific Documents — Invitation letter (for business/family visits), admission letter (for student visas), marriage certificate (for spouse visas), or NOC from employer.

For detailed document checklists, visit our Visa Document Checklist 2026 blog post or the Visa Requirements page for country-specific requirements. You can also refer to country-specific guides like our US visa Interview Tips, UK visa processing times, or Schengen visa guide.

FRRO Mumbai: Services for Foreign Nationals

The Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) in Mumbai provides essential services for foreign nationals residing in or visiting Mumbai. Key services include:

  • Visa Registration — Foreign nationals staying in India beyond 180 days must register within 14 days of arrival.
  • Visa Extension — Extend your tourist, business, or employment visa while in India.
  • Visa Conversion — Convert one visa type to another (e.g., tourist to employment visa) subject to eligibility.
  • Exit Permit — Permission to exit India for foreign nationals who have lost their passport or overstayed their visa.
  • OCI/PIO Services — Overseas Citizen of India card applications, renewals, and related services.
  • Student Visa Related Services — Registration, extension, and bonafide certificate issuance for foreign students studying in Mumbai.

All FRRO services in Mumbai are now managed through the government's e-FRRO online portal, which allows you to submit applications, upload documents, make payments, and track progress without visiting the office in person for most services.

Tips for a Smooth Visa Application Experience in Mumbai

  • Book appointments well in advance — US visa interview wait times in Mumbai can reach 2–3 months during peak seasons. Check current wait times on the official US Embassy wait times page.
  • Organise documents in the order specified — Most centres provide a document checklist. Arrange your documents in the same order as the checklist for faster processing.
  • Carry originals and photocopies — Take originals for verification and a separate set of photocopies for submission. The centre may not have photocopy services readily available.
  • Arrive 15–20 minutes before your appointment — Late arrivals may be turned away and asked to reschedule. Security screening at consulates can take additional time.
  • Dress appropriately — Business casual attire is recommended for consular interviews. Avoid wearing hats, sunglasses, or headphones.
  • Do not bring electronic devices — Mobile phones, smartwatches, laptops, and bags are not allowed inside most consulate premises. Most centres have locker facilities nearby for a fee.
  • Verify visa centre location beforehand — Multiple centres operate in BKC. Confirm the exact building and floor on your appointment confirmation letter.

Frequently Asked Questions About Visa Services in Mumbai

Where is the VFS Global visa application centre in Mumbai?

VFS Global Mumbai is located at Unit 101, 1st Floor, A Wing, TCG Financial Centre, BKC, Bandra East, Mumbai — 400051. The centre processes visa applications for the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and several European countries. Appointment booking is mandatory through the VFS Global website.

How do I book a US visa appointment in Mumbai?

US visa appointments in Mumbai are booked through the official UStraveldocs website. You must first complete the DS-160 form online, pay the visa fee, and then schedule your appointment at the US Consulate in BKC. Check current appointment wait times on the official U.S. Embassy wait times page.

What is the processing time for UK visa from Mumbai?

UK visa processing from Mumbai typically takes 15 to 30 working days for standard applications. Priority service (5 working days) and Super Priority service (next working day) are available at the VFS Global centre in BKC for an additional fee of GBP 500 and GBP 800 respectively. Processing starts after biometric submission and may take longer if additional verification is needed.

Do I need to visit the visa application centre in person in Mumbai?

Yes, most visa types require in-person attendance for biometric data collection (fingerprints and photograph). This applies to US, UK, Canada, Australia, Schengen, and many other visa categories. However, some countries offer e-Visa or visa-free entry where in-person visits are not required — check country-specific requirements on our Visa Guide.

How do I register with FRRO in Mumbai?

Foreign nationals must register with FRRO Mumbai within 14 days of arrival if staying beyond 180 days. Registration is done online through the official e-FRRO portal. You need a valid passport with Indian visa, photographs, proof of address in India, and the applicable fee. Once the online application is approved, you may need to visit the FRRO office at Crawford Market for biometric verification.

What are the visa application centre timings in Mumbai?

VFS Global (BKC): Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. US Consulate (BKC): Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM (by appointment only). Passport Seva Kendras: Mon–Sat, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. FRRO Mumbai: Mon–Fri, 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM. All centres are closed on public holidays.

Can I apply for a Schengen visa in Mumbai?

Yes, Mumbai has consulates and visa application centres for several Schengen countries including France, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Netherlands, and Spain. Most Schengen visa applications from Mumbai are processed through VFS Global or TLScontact depending on the destination country. See our complete Schengen visa guide for detailed information.

What is the cheapest visa option from Mumbai?

Visa fees vary significantly by destination. e-Visa options (available for countries like Turkey, Singapore, Sri Lanka, and Kenya) are often the most affordable at $20–$60. Thailand Visa on Arrival costs THB 2,000 (~â‚¹4,800). Among Western destinations, Schengen visa (€80) and UK Standard Visitor visa (GBP 115) are relatively economical compared to US ($185) or Australia (AUD 190) visas.

How long does Canada visa processing take from Mumbai?

Canada visa processing from Mumbai typically takes 2 to 8 weeks depending on the visa category and application volume. Visitor Visa (TRV) applications are generally processed within 2–4 weeks for straightforward cases. Study Permit and Work Permit applications may take 4–8 weeks. Biometrics must be submitted at VFS Global BKC before processing begins. Track your application on the official IRCC website.

Do I need travel insurance for visa applications from Mumbai?

Travel insurance is mandatory for Schengen visa applications (minimum €30,000 coverage). It is strongly recommended for US, UK, Canada, and Australia visa applications as it demonstrates preparedness and addresses consular concerns about medical emergencies abroad. Most visa application centres in Mumbai offer travel insurance purchase as an add-on service.

Can I track my visa application status online?

Yes, most visa application centres and embassies provide online tracking. For VFS Global applications, use the tracking feature on VFS Global's website. For US visas, check on the US Travel Docs portal. For IRCC applications (Canada), use the GCKey or online tracking tool. The reference number from your application receipt is required for tracking.
