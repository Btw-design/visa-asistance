Tips for Event Travel Planning

  • Apply for your visa at least 3-6 months before the event–peak seasons cause appointment delays.
  • Check if the event requires a specific ticket or invitation letter for visa applications (especially business conferences).
  • Book accommodation early–prices surge significantly during major events like the Olympics and World Cup.
  • Consider travel insurance that covers event cancellation, medical emergencies, and trip interruptions.
  • For multi-country events like the FIFA World Cup and Cricket World Cup, ensure your visa covers all destination countries.
  • Keep digital and printed copies of your visa, passport, event tickets, and travel insurance.
  • Check entry requirements (vaccinations, COVID-19 rules) that may apply in addition to visa requirements.

During major events like the FIFA World Cup, Olympics, or Cricket World Cup, embassies and visa application centers experience significantly higher volumes, leading to longer processing times and limited appointment availability. For business conferences like CES or Cannes Lions, you typically need: a valid passport (6+ months validity), completed visa application, conference registration confirmation or invitation letter from organizers, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, bank statements (last 3-6 months), employment letter, and travel insurance.
