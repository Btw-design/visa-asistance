Who Needs a Visa & Who Is It Required For?

Understanding visa requirements is the first step to international travel. Here is a clear breakdown.

Tourists & Leisure Travellers

Anyone travelling abroad for vacation, sightseeing, visiting family, or recreational purposes needs a Tourist visa unless their nationality is visa-exempt for the destination country. We assist with tourist visa applications for all major destinations.

Business Travellers

Professionals attending meetings, conferences, trade shows, or exploring business opportunities abroad require a Business visa. This covers corporate visits, client meetings, and exploratory business trips.

Visit Visa Applicants

Individuals visiting friends or family members who are residents or citizens of another country need a Visit visa. This also includes those attending weddings, family events, or short-term stays with relatives.

Work Visa Seekers

Professionals who already have a confirmed job offer from an overseas employer need a Work visa. We process work visa applications only when you have a valid job offer letter and all required employer documents.

Students

Individuals accepted into educational institutions abroad need a Student visa. We provide documentation guidance to ensure your student visa application meets all requirements.

Transit Passengers

If your international flight has a layover in a country that requires a transit visa, we help you apply. Some nationalities need transit visas even for short airport stopovers.

Tourist Visa

A Tourist visa is for individuals travelling abroad for leisure, sightseeing, holidays, or visiting family. We assist with the entire application process including document checklist, form filling, appointment booking, and submission guidance.

Key requirements: Valid passport (6+ months validity), recent photographs, bank statements (last 3-6 months), travel itinerary, flight and hotel bookings, travel insurance, and cover letter explaining purpose of visit.

Business Visa

A Business visa is for professionals travelling abroad for business meetings, conferences, trade exhibitions, client visits, or exploring investment opportunities. We help prepare the required documentation including company invitation letters and business proof.

Key requirements: Valid passport, business invitation letter from the overseas company, company registration documents, recent photographs, bank statements, IT returns, travel itinerary, and travel insurance.

Countries we cover: USA Business Visa UK Business Visa Canada Business Visa Australia Business Visa Schengen Business Visa China Business Visa Japan Business Visa Singapore Business Visa UAE Business Visa Thailand Business Visa Turkey Business Visa Germany Business Visa France Business Visa Italy Business Visa Netherlands Business Visa

Visit Visa

A Visit visa is meant for individuals travelling to meet family members, attend weddings, family events, or visit friends who are residents or citizens of another country. This category covers personal visits that are not strictly tourism or business.

Key requirements: Valid passport, invitation letter from the host, proof of host's residency/citizenship, relationship proof, recent photographs, bank statements, travel insurance, and return flight bookings.

Countries we cover: USA Visit Visa UK Visit Visa Canada Visit Visa Australia Visit Visa Schengen Visit Visa India Visit Visa UAE Visit Visa

Work Visa

We process Work visa applications for professionals who already have a confirmed job offer from an overseas employer. Important: We do not provide job search, job placement, or recruitment services. We only handle the visa application process when you already possess a valid job offer letter and all required documents from your employer.

Required documents: Valid passport (6+ months validity), signed employment contract or job offer letter from the overseas employer, employer sponsorship documents, educational certificates and professional credentials, proof of relevant work experience, recent photographs, police clearance certificate, medical examination reports (as required by the destination country), and financial documents as specified.
