Photo Guidelines

Quick Tips

  • Use a plain white or light grey background
  • Face centered, eyes clearly visible
  • No shadows on face or background
  • No hair covering eyebrows or eyes
  • Neutral expression, mouth closed
  • No red-eye or filters
  • Photo must be recent (usually ≤6 months)

Photo Checklist

Print this checklist before your photo session.

Correct size (mm/inches as specified)
White/light background (no patterns)
Face centered, proper head size
Eyes visible, no hair covering
Neutral expression, mouth closed
No glasses or approved frames only
No shadows, uniform lighting
Correct file format (JPEG/PNG)
File size within limit
Photo taken within last 6 months
