Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visa applications and our services.

My Visa Assistance provides comprehensive visa guides, requirements information, document checklists, and expert application support for tourist, business, student, work, and transit visas for all countries worldwide.

Browse our Visa Guide section, select your destination country, review the specific visa requirements and guides, then submit your inquiry through our contact form. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

Visa processing times vary by country and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 3-15 business days. Check our country-specific visa guides for detailed processing times and expedited options.

Common documents include a valid passport (6+ months validity), completed application form, passport photographs, travel itinerary, hotel bookings, bank statements, and employment letter. Requirements vary by country and visa type.

No legitimate visa service can guarantee approval, as visa decisions are made solely by embassy officials. However, we help ensure your application is complete and accurate, significantly increasing your chances of approval.
