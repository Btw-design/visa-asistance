Egypt Visa Requirements 2026

Complete requirements for obtaining a visa for Egypt, including document checklists, fees, and eligibility criteria.

Egypt Visa Requirements - Overview

Egypt visa requires a valid passport, completed application form, passport-sized photograph, and proof of travel arrangements.

Required Documents

The following documents are generally required for a Egypt visa application:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Confirmed flight booking
  • Hotel reservation
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Travel insurance

Note: Additional documents may be required depending on the visa type and your specific circumstances.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a Egypt visa, applicants must generally meet the following criteria:

  • Hold a valid passport with sufficient validity
  • Have a clear purpose of visit (tourism, business, study, etc.)
  • Demonstrate sufficient financial means for the duration of stay
  • Show strong ties to home country (employment, family, property)
  • Have no immigration violations or criminal record
  • Meet health requirements (may require medical examination)

Application Process

  1. Check if you need a visa for Egypt based on your nationality
  2. Determine the correct visa type for your purpose of travel
  3. Gather all required documents listed above
  4. Complete the visa application form accurately
  5. Pay the applicable visa fee
  6. Submit your application at the embassy/consulate or online
  7. Attend visa interview if required
  8. Wait for processing and collect your visa

Important Notes

Before You Apply

  • Visa requirements may change without notice. Always verify with the official embassy website.
  • Processing times vary and may take longer during peak travel seasons.
  • Incomplete applications with missing documents are likely to be rejected.
  • Providing false information can result in visa refusal and future travel bans.
  • Check if your nationality is eligible for visa-free travel or visa on arrival.

A passport is a government-issued identification document that proves your identity and citizenship for international travel. A visa is an official authorisation endorsed on your passport (or issued electronically) that grants you permission to enter, stay, or transit through a foreign country for a specific purpose and duration.

The 6-month passport validity rule requires your passport to be valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay in the destination country.
